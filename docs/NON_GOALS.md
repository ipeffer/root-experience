# Non-Goals

The following are intentionally excluded from the ROOT Experience MVP:

1. **Payment orchestration** inside this application
2. **Checkout replacement** for Shopify
3. **Automatic booking confirmation** or calendar locking
4. **Dynamic pricing engine** for experiences
5. **Complex personalization/ML recommendations**
6. **Native mobile apps**
7. **Back-office dashboard with role management**
8. **External CRM or ERP deep integrations**

## Why This Matters
Maintaining these boundaries keeps the MVP fast to ship, easier to maintain, and aligned with the immediate business outcome: qualified leads and booking requests.
